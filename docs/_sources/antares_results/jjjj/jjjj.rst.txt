pp → jjjj
=========

Six-gluon one-loop amplitudes (with a gluon in the loop) computed in
collaboration with `Daniel
Maître <https://inspirehep.net/authors/1021220>`__. The quark loop is in
principle known because the N=4 and N=1 SUSY amplitudes are known.

Published in
`10.1007/JHEP07(2019)123 <https://inspirehep.net/literature/1728738>`__
